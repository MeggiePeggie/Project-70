Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

The students at our school did an amazing job on this debating competition! It was such a proud momment for the school. This event would be one that the school would remember forever. Pollution is a big problem in our world and it's nice to see our next generation of people bringing these topics to our attention.

Inter School Football Competition
Came second in inter school football competition

Inter School Sports is a fun, active activity for schools. Inter School Sports let's our school meet other schools in a friendly competition. It's a fun way to make new friends and a great way to keep active. We won second place for football. It's a great acheivement that our school has earned. The other school was very encouraging and were very good sports which made winning even better!

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won second prize in inter school Science Project competition

We are very proud that we got to participate in this competition. Over 10 schools were competing, and all of them had amazing science projects. Despite the tough competition, we won second place! It was nearly a draw with another school, but we got the prize! We only did our best but that's all you need to win.
